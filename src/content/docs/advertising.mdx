## Advertising intent

MarkdownPic is being designed with future monetization in mind, but the site is not intended to become an ad-heavy reference property.

The preferred direction is:

- strong editorial content first,
- limited and well-positioned sponsor inventory later,
- and clear disclosure where commercial relationships exist.

## Likely future inventory

The most natural fit for future commercial placements includes:

- Markdown editors and PKM tools,
- documentation platforms,
- static-site and CMS products,
- image/CDN services,
- note-taking tools,
- AI workflow products,
- and Markdown-to-image adjacent utilities.

## Placement principle

The most valuable pages for future advertising are likely to be:

- editor comparisons,
- publishing workflow guides,
- syntax pages with strong problem intent,
- and tool-adjacent guides with direct execution value.

Those are also the pages that need the most restraint. Commercial inventory should never make the reference layer harder to trust.
