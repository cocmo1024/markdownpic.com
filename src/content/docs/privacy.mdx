## Overview

MarkdownPic is a content site plus a separate tool experience.

This policy is intended to explain, in practical terms, how privacy is approached across:

- `markdownpic.com`
- `tool.markdownpic.com`

## Content site

The main site is a standard documentation and reference website. Typical logs, analytics, performance metrics, and hosting-layer security data may be processed to keep the site running, understand site performance, and improve content quality.

If advertising, sponsorship, or analytics tooling is added later, those relationships should remain disclosed in the relevant policy pages.

## Tool site

The tool is designed around a browser-first workflow. Depending on the tool version and the browser environment, draft content, image data, and rendering state may remain local to the browser for usability and recovery purposes.

The tool should not be treated as a place to paste sensitive secrets, credentials, or proprietary content unless you are comfortable with the local browser environment and the related operational risks.

## Email and contact

If you send a message to `cqq365@gmail.com`, the email itself and any content you include may be retained for support, editorial follow-up, or business correspondence.

## Cookies and analytics

The site or tool may use analytics, caching, CDN, or advertising-related technologies to improve delivery, measure usage, or support future monetization. Actual implementation can change over time, so this policy should be read as a practical overview rather than a promise of zero third-party services.

## Your control

You remain responsible for what you paste into the tool, what files you export, and what information you share by email.

If you do not want local browser storage, analytics, or future ad-related processing, the most reliable controls remain your browser settings, privacy tooling, and your choice not to use the relevant feature.

Questions about privacy can be sent to:
