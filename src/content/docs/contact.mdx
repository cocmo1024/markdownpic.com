## Contact routes

MarkdownPic currently handles editorial, business, and tool-related contact through a single mailbox:

- `cqq365@gmail.com`

## What to include

To get a useful response faster, include the context that matters:

- the page or URL involved,
- the Markdown or rendering problem,
- the editor, platform, or workflow involved,
- and whether the issue is about the site, the tool, or a partnership request.

## Best use cases for contact

- Editorial corrections or factual issues
- Partnership or sponsorship inquiries
- Tool workflow feedback
- Broken-link or migration issues
- Suggestions for new Markdown topics or comparison pages
