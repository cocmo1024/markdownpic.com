## Disclosure

MarkdownPic may eventually use affiliate links on selected pages where a tool, service, or product is relevant to the topic being discussed.

If that happens:

- the relationship should be disclosed clearly,
- the page should still be editorially useful without a click,
- and the presence of an affiliate relationship should not determine whether a tool is covered or how it is evaluated.

## Editorial boundary

MarkdownPic is intended to optimize for long-term trust, not short-term conversion tricks. If an affiliate relationship would make a recommendation less honest, the recommendation should not be bent to fit the affiliate relationship.
